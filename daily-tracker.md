## 🎯 Daily/weekly time slots (sacred)

| Slot | Hours | Track |
|---|---|---|
| **Morning 6:30-8:30** | ~12 hr/wk | **DSA daily** (per day blocks below) → switches to **LLD daily problem** from Sep 13 |
| **Commute (both ways)** | ~6 hr/wk | **LLD concept videos** (daily section per week below) → switches to **HLD/system design videos** from Sep 13 |
| **3 non-class weeknights × 1.5 hr** | ~4.5 hr/wk | **Project** (weekly milestone from week header) |
| **Weekend (~5 hr each day)** | ~10 hr/wk | **Project** + 1 spaced-DSA-review slot |

**Rules:**
- Never sacrifice morning DSA for project work. Project lives in evenings + weekends only.
- One full off-period/week (Sunday afternoon recommended).
- Project plan reference: `PROJECT_PLAN.md` Section 5 · LLD curriculum: `lld_content_index.md`

## 📖 Legend

**Track icons (used in daily blocks):** 🧠 DSA · 📚 LLD concept · 🧩 LLD problem (Phase 2) · 🎓 HLD video (Phase 2) · 🚀 Project · 📨 Apply

- **`### 📅 Daily`** — concrete day-by-day tasks (what to do today)
